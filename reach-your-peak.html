Meet Theo.

He loves the outdoors and anything that can get his adrenaline going. Theo wants to finish school, but he finds it harder to stay focused and complete work he's not interested in.
Theo wanted to study a Certificate II in Outdoor Recreation, but his school doesn't offer this. So instead, he is told to choose between Engineering and Hospitality. 

*Story for illustration purposes only

NEXT

Meet Peter.

He runs an outdoor recreation centre located on the Sunshine Coast.
Peter is constantly replacing talented group leaders and guides as in the quieter times during the year; there is not enough work to keep them on.

NEXT

Theo chooses Hospitality because most of his friends think it’s the easy option ... 

He soon gets bored and attends the class but can't be bothered to hand any of the necessary work in.

AN ALTERNATIVE ENDING

Then, Peter discovered Reach Your Peak

He nominates his best casual leader to be trained to deliver the Certificate II in Outdoor Recreation.
Reach Your Peak connects Peter directly with the community where they advertise an available student place.

NEXT

Another job is created.

After offering Theo a student position, Peter uses one of his best casual leaders to deliver the Cert II and Theo becomes a trained outdoor recreation leader.
Peter's outdoor recreation centre is paid for the training.

NEXT

It's no surprise Peter uses Reach Your Peak as a way of funding Matt's position. As the camp always needs group leaders or assistant guides and support staff, having students is now the perfect way to see and recruit future talent.

Theo shines and is offered a traineeship as an assistant activity guide.
