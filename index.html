Our Drive and Mission

We are Committed to Promoting Change!

Our strategies are long-term and emphasise generational change, inter-generational cooperation, community and neighbourhood empowerment, cross-community networking, resourcing, and support.

  • We are committed to the empowerment of people to be all that they can be.
  • We are committed to the mental health and wellbeing of individuals and communities.
  • We are committed to building a community of influence.
  • We are committed to each other!
